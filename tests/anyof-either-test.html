(set: $a to (array: 2,4,6,8)) (either: $a) (= 2,4,6,8 - `(either:)` treats the collection as 1 value) (any-of: $a) (= 2, 4, 6 or 8 - `(any-of:)` flattens the collection) (either: 1,3,5) (= 1, 3 or 5) (any-of: 1,3,5) (= 1, 3 or 5 - `(any-of:)` is special-cased to act like `(either:)` when given non-collection(s).)
